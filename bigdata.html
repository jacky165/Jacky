Big Data

 Reflection: For this presentation, I was required to research an innovation that 
 uses big data in some manner. I chose physical activity trackers such as Fitbit because 
 to use these for large-scale medical diagnosing a lot of space would be needed to store the data.
 Though they have not yet been used for this purpose several researchers believe we are moving
 towards making them highly widespread beneficial devices.
